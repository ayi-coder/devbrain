C

Clustered Graph — nodes that build connections

Concepts are nodes. As you complete them, visible connection lines appear between related concepts across categories. The map grows denser as you learn more.

Solid lines = connections you've unlocked. Dashed = coming soon.
✓ Shows relationships visually  ✓ Grows with you  ✗ Can feel overwhelming without enough context
